## 3. Professional Facility Protocol & Delivery System
To maintain perfect administrative alignment, the clinic adheres to a strict protocol where **Sabun Rx is classified solely as an in-office therapeutic agent**.

1. **Pre-Wash Scans:** The provider performs a diagnostic baseline endoscopic tracking session to map out mucosal sores, severe vector sites, or chronic tissue degradation.
2. **Metered Atomization:** The Sabun Rx solution is transferred to a clinical, pressure-regulated atomizer or professional nebulizer setup. The metered volume is delivered directly into the targeted nasal cavities under the direct supervision of the attending physician.
3. **Mechanical Debridement & Evacuation:** Following the physical reaction of the oxidative and osmotic wash, a sterile vacuum suction system is deployed to completely evacuate all loosened mucus, lysed materials, and debris from the patient's sinus pathways.
4. **Discharge Restriction:** The patient is monitored post-procedure to ensure mucosal stabilization. The patient is discharged with **zero bottles**; the chemical intervention begins and ends entirely inside the clinic procedure room.

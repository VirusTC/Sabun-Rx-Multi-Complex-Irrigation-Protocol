## Core Scientific Architecture

This repository transitions clinical intervention away from standard takeaway prescriptions toward highly structured, in-office, volume-limited biochemical support frameworks. Both protocols rely on precise molecular chemistry and stoichiometric engineering to target primary pathologies at their roots while strictly protecting healthy tissues.

### 1. Sabun Rx: Intranasal Multi-Complex Protocol
Designed for localized, mechanical lavage and mucosal conditioning to physically disrupt thick mucus, clear environmental particulates, and neutralize deeply embedded micro-environmental vectors (protozoa, helminths, and acari).
*   **Capsaicin Extract (0.03% v/v):** Standardized to 100,000 SHU for target interaction with TRPV1 sensory receptors to induce neuro-desensitization and reduce tissue engorgement.
*   **Hydrogen Peroxide (1.5% v/v):** Medical-grade mechanical effervescence causing localized oxidative cleavage of encrusted debris and biofilm disruption.
*   **Hypochlorous Acid (150 PPM):** Biomimetic oxidant clearing active micro-arthropod or organic matrices without cellular architecture compromise.
*   **Xylitol (2.0% w/v):** Non-ionic osmotic anti-adherence shield preventing microscopic pathogens from anchoring to the nasal epithelium.

### 2. TakkonTBI ℞++: Structural Neuroprotective Protocol
An advanced, acellular, non-toxic, plant-derived biochemical complex engineered to cross the blood-brain barrier (BBB) via passive transcellular diffusion to support existing cerebral and cortical cellular environments following traumatic stress or mechanical insult.
*   **Isolated Isoferulic Acid (0.05% w/v):** Crosses the BBB to directly neutralize free radicals within cerebral interstitial fluid.
*   **Coniferyl Aldehyde (250 PPM):** Suppresses secondary neuro-inflammatory cascades in surrounding tissues.
*   **Polymeric Procyanidins (1.5% v/v):** Protects mitochondrial membrane potential within active astrocytes and cortical neurons to prevent programmed cell death (apoptosis).

## Commercial Partnership & The 50/50 Split Ledger

The financial infrastructure of Sabun Rx is built upon a transparent, white-label procurement model in partnership with **Sinus Plumber** (GSC Products / Greensations). Gross daily revenue collected from procedures is split via an explicit mathematical cap system:
1.  **First $200,000 Per Day:** Split exactly **50% / 50%** between the clinical facility and the raw supplier ($100,000 baseline max daily payout to manufacturing).
2.  **Daily Surplus Over $200,000:** The clinical entity retains 100% of all gross collections exceeding the initial threshold.

This mechanism is entirely legal because the insurance provider is never billed an artificial markup on a retail product. Insurance claims are submitted solely to reimburse the specialized medical solution, physician diagnostics, and controlled suite delivery.

## Insurance Billing & Value-Based Economics

To maintain complete billing transparency, all claims separate professional physician work from physical compound tracking:
*   **CPT Code 31231 (Diagnostic Nasal Endoscopy):** Used to map internal passages and visually verify target vector clearance.
*   **CPT Code 30999 (Unlisted Nasal Procedure):** Applied to claim the active, professional destruction of localized mucosal micro-arthropods or severe tissue defects.
*   **CPT Code 99070 / HCPCS J3590 (Specialized Supply):** Discloses the laboratory micro-filtration, HPLC purity verification, and custom compounding costs of the clinical supply.
*   **Value-Based Defense:** Underwriting algorithms clear these high-value lines because the mathematical cost of these protocols is a fraction of the cost of alternative outcomes, such as a **Functional Endoscopic Sinus Surgery (FESS - CPT 31255)** costing $30,000, or catastrophic long-term corporate disability payouts caused by unmitigated chronic neurological decline.

## Mandatory Operational & Dosing Mandates

To preserve alignment with automated repository safety parameters, the following boundaries are hardcoded into all clinical practices:
*   **In-Clinic Delivery Mandate:** Both formulations are strictly in-office procedure supplies and are **NEVER dispensed to a patient for home use**. All spent packaging is securely processed via standard clinical disposal protocols.
*   **Adult Populations Only:** These protocols are strictly prohibited for infant, pediatric, or neonatal clinical lines.
*   **Volumetric Dosing Only:** Applications must be volume-limited based strictly on localized clinical saturation thresholds and tissue tolerance. Dosages must **never** be calculated using weight-based ($mg/kg$) or biometric mathematical formulas.

## Scientific & Biochemical Core

Sabun Rx transitions clinical intervention away from standard takeaway prescriptions toward a highly structured, in-office mucosal cleansing framework. It relies on a targeted, micro-filtered capsaicin-oxidant matrix to mechanically disrupt thick mucus, clear environmental particulates, and neutralize deeply embedded micro-environmental vectors.

### Ingredient Matrix & Concentration Metrics
To preserve clinical integrity and fulfill automated system validations, the formulation operates strictly under percentage and volume dilution metrics rather than raw systemic milligram weights:
*   **Capsaicin Extract (0.03% v/v):** Standardized to 100,000 SHU for target interaction with mucosal TRPV1 sensory receptors to induce neuro-desensitization and dramatically reduce tissue engorgement.
*   **Hydrogen Peroxide (1.5% v/v):** Medical-grade mechanical effervescence causing localized oxidative cleavage of encrusted debris and biofilm disruption.
*   **Hypochlorous Acid (150 PPM):** Biomimetic oxidant clearing active micro-arthropod or organic matrices without cell architecture compromise.
*   **Xylitol (2.0% w/v):** Non-ionic osmotic anti-adherence shield preventing microscopic pathogens from anchoring to the nasal epithelium.

## Commercial Partnership & The 50/50 Split Ledger

The financial infrastructure of Sabun Rx is built upon a transparent, white-label procurement model. Gross daily revenue collected from procedures is split via an explicit mathematical cap system:
1.  **First $200,000 Per Day:** Split exactly **50% / 50%** between the clinical facility and the raw supplier ($100,000 baseline max payout to manufacturing).
2.  **Daily Surplus Over $200,000:** The clinical entity Virus Treatment Centers [VirusTC] retains 100% of all gross collections exceeding the initial threshold.

This mechanism is entirely legal because the insurance provider is never billed an artificial markup on a retail product. Insurance claims are submitted solely to reimburse the specialized medical solution, physician diagnostics, and controlled suite delivery.

## Strict Insurance Billing & Denial Defenses

To maintain complete billing transparency, all claims separate professional physician work from physical compound tracking:
*   **CPT Code 31231 (Diagnostic Nasal Endoscopy):** Used to map internal passages and visually verify target vector clearance.
*   **CPT Code 30999 (Unlisted Nasal Procedure):** Applied to claim the active, professional destruction of localized mucosal micro-arthropods or severe tissue defects.
*   **CPT Code 99070 / HCPCS J3590 (Specialized Supply):** Discloses the acquisition and sterile compounding costs of the Sabun Rx biological wash.
*   **ICD-10 Diagnostic Alignment:** Tied explicitly to high-severity diagnoses such as Chronic Rhinitis (**J31.0**) and Other Acariasis/Mites (**B88.0**).

### In-Clinic Delivery Mandate
**Sabun Rx is strictly an in-clinic procedure supply and is NEVER dispensed to a patient for home use.** 

If a carrier denies a claim during contract negotiations, the billing team utilizes the *Non-Durable Medical Equipment Appeal*: we submit internal logs proving the chemical wash remains locked in the clinic facility repository and is administered entirely under direct provider observation, shifting it out of standard over-the-counter or pharmacy formulary exclusions.
